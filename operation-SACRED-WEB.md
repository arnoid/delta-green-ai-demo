## Theme
This is 2026. A new COVID variant emerges. Russo-Ukrainian war is a stalemate. The US is in preparation to invade Iran as Iran blocks oil shimpments in Ormuz strait.  World is on the brink of economic collapse.

## Core Premise & Setting
It is March 2026. A new COVID variant — designated HCoV-NX3 by the WHO and 'Lazarus' in classified DHS memos — has begun spreading through pediatric wards across the American Midwest, killing children aged 4–12 within 72 hours of symptom onset. The CDC's preliminary genomic sequencing flags something impossible: embedded within the virus's spike protein is a non-coding sequence that does not match any known biological organism, terrestrial or otherwise. The sequence is self-replicating, non-functional by any measurable metric, and yet perfectly conserved across every sampled strain — as if it was placed there deliberately.

Against this backdrop, the Russo-Ukrainian war has ground into a frozen, attrition-soaked stalemate along the Dnipro line, with both sides hemorrhaging soldiers and treasure into the mud. Iran, emboldened by the geopolitical distraction, has effectively closed the Strait of Hormuz to international shipping, triggering an oil shock that has sent fuel prices past $12 a gallon in the United States. The Federal Reserve has run out of tools. Three G7 nations are quietly rationing food staples. The US 5th Fleet is steaming toward the Persian Gulf, and every Pentagon war-game ends the same way: a regional conflict that metastasizes into something the world cannot come back from.

Delta Green's Agents are briefed through a dead-drop in a Walmart parking lot in Wichita, Kansas — an encrypted USB drive tucked inside a sealed box of generic ibuprofen left at register 9. The message is terse: six children across three states have died of HCoV-NX3 complications. All six were adopted from a single private international adoption agency — Hearthstone Futures, LLC — headquartered in a beige office park in Overland Park, Kansas. The agency processed adoptions from Ukraine, Iran, and Syria between 2019 and 2024. All six children share no bloodline, no geographic origin, and no common medical history. They share only one thing: each child, in their final hours, reportedly spoke in a language no one around them recognized, described by attending nurses as 'wet' and 'layered, like two voices stacked.' One nurse recorded seven seconds of it on her personal phone before her phone erased itself.

The Setup: Delta Green's own Handler — designated Agent NORA, a mid-level FEMA logistics coordinator with sunken eyes and trembling hands — is not what she appears. Agent NORA has been feeding the cell to a rival faction within the U.S. intelligence apparatus, a black-budget unit called CARDINAL LOOP operating under the Joint Chiefs, which has known about the non-coding sequence for eighteen months. CARDINAL LOOP did not discover the sequence in HCoV-NX3. They put it there. The children were not randomly selected by a virus. They were selected by Hearthstone Futures, which is a CARDINAL LOOP front. The children were specifically adopted because they were orphaned survivors of unnatural incursion sites — Chernobyl's exclusion zone, an unnamed village in Syria's Deir ez-Zor governorate, a flooded district outside Ahvaz, Iran — places where something old and non-human had already touched the local population at a genomic level generations ago. CARDINAL LOOP believed these children carried a latent, inheritable biological 'key' — a remnant corruption left by an unnatural presence that predates recorded history — and that HCoV-NX3 was engineered as a vector to activate it. The Agents are not being sent to investigate a disease outbreak. They are being sent to be caught at the scene of CARDINAL LOOP's containment operation — a pre-planned cleanup of the children, the nurses, the adoption records, and anyone else who got too close — so that Delta Green takes the institutional blame when it unravels.

The Horror: What CARDINAL LOOP does not understand, and what the Agents will discover at terrible cost, is that the non-coding sequence is not a key they engineered. They found it, copied it, and weaponized it — but the original source, recovered from a bore sample taken 4.2 kilometers below the Ahvaz oil field in 2019, is not inert. It is not biological. It is a corrupted unnatural inscription — something that was never meant to exist inside living tissue — and the children are not dying from the virus. They are dying because the sequence, once active, begins rewriting the child's neurological architecture to serve as a receiver. A receiver for something vast, patient, and deeply wrong that has been broadcasting for longer than the human species has existed. The six dead children were the first successful connections. Their final words were not a language. They were a signal, looping back. And somewhere in the oil fields beneath a burning Strait of Hormuz, the bore site is still open.

# ☣️ THE VECTOR: SEQUENCE NX-PRIME

The non-coding sequence — designated **NX-Prime** in recovered CARDINAL LOOP documentation, referred to in the Ahvaz bore sample field notes only as *"the inscription"* — is not a pathogen. It is not biological in any meaningful sense. It is closer to a *carved instruction*, the way a groove pressed into vinyl is not music but becomes music when the right needle touches it.

HCoV-NX3 is the needle.

The virus itself is a mundane, engineered coronavirus — sophisticated, lethal, but explicable. CARDINAL LOOP's virologists spent eighteen months grafting NX-Prime into the spike protein's non-coding region, believing it was inert passenger data — a biological dog-tag that would survive replication. They were half right. It does survive replication. It survives *perfectly*. What they did not understand is that NX-Prime is not data. It is a *process*. Once it crosses the blood-brain barrier and achieves sufficient neurological density — a threshold reached only in specific subjects with the latent genomic predisposition the Hearthstone children carry — it stops being a passenger and becomes a *tenant*.

---

## 🧬 Stages of Exposure & Transmission

Exposure is not contagious in the conventional sense. NX-Prime requires two simultaneous conditions to activate:

> **Condition A — The Predisposition:** The subject must carry the latent genomic remnant left by prior unnatural contact in their ancestral line. This is vanishingly rare. The Hearthstone children were specifically selected because they carry it. Ordinary HCoV-NX3 infection in a non-predisposed subject produces a severe but standard respiratory illness. Fatal in some cases. Explicable. Containable. A *distraction*.

> **Condition B — Viral Load Threshold:** The subject must sustain sufficient HCoV-NX3 infection long enough for NX-Prime to replicate to neurological-crossing density. In predisposed subjects, this occurs within 36–48 hours of symptom onset. In non-predisposed subjects, NX-Prime degrades harmlessly within the immune response.

The Agents are, with overwhelming probability, **non-predisposed**. They cannot activate. They can, however, be *adjacent to activation* — and proximity to a child in Stage III or Stage IV exposure carries its own category of cost that has nothing to do with virology.

---

## 📶 Stages of Infection in Predisposed Subjects

### **Stage I — Carrier (Hours 0–24)**
Asymptomatic viral replication. The child presents with mild fatigue and low-grade fever. Indistinguishable from a common cold. NX-Prime begins passive replication. No behavioral anomalies. This stage is entirely invisible without genomic sequencing.

> *Nothing to see. That is the point. The children went to school. They went home. They were held.*

---

### **Stage II — Resonance Onset (Hours 24–48)**
Fever spikes. Respiratory distress begins. The child becomes unusually quiet — not lethargic, but *listening*. Parents and nurses describe the child turning their head toward walls, toward floors, toward no visible stimulus. REM sleep becomes impossible; the child does not sleep, but does not appear distressed by the inability. EEG readings during this stage, in the two cases where hospital staff thought to run them, show a pattern that neurologists flag as "structured artifact" — regular, rhythmic, and *externally consistent across both subjects despite different hospitals and different states*. The pattern does not match any known neurological state. It matches, when the Agents eventually access the raw data, the electromagnetic signature of the Ahvaz bore site at 4.2 kilometers depth.

> *The child is not generating the signal. The child is learning to receive it.*

---

### **Stage III — Architectural Rewrite (Hours 48–66)**
The NX-Prime sequence achieves critical neurological density. The child's higher cognitive functions begin to be rerouted — not destroyed, rerouted — around a new processing architecture that has no human analogue. The child becomes hyper-responsive to stimuli no one else can perceive. They react to sounds in frequencies outside human hearing range. They flinch from corners of empty rooms. They reach toward ceilings.

The speech begins here.

What attending nurses describe as "layered" and "wet" is, on the seven-second recording the nurse captured before her phone self-erased, not a language with grammar or syntax. It is a *carrier wave rendered in human vocalization* — the child's vocal cords being used as a transceiver. The "two voices stacked" quality is literal: one voice is the child's own, producing sounds the human throat is not designed to make. The second voice is something else entirely, using the first as a medium.

The child is not speaking. The child is *routing a signal back to its source.* Each iteration is slightly longer. Each iteration is slightly clearer. By the end of Stage III, the child is no longer initiating the sound. The child is being *played*.

---

### **Stage IV — Connection & Terminus (Hours 66–72)**
The rewrite completes. For a period of minutes — ranging from four to, in the final documented case, thirty-one — the child achieves full reception. What they receive cannot be characterized. Witnesses describe the child becoming absolutely still. Their eyes do not dilate or contract regardless of light stimulus. Their temperature drops to 93°F without cardiovascular distress. And then they speak — clearly, in a register that is recognizably childlike, in the stacked vocalization — for the last time.

Then they die.

Cause of death on every certificate reads: *acute respiratory failure secondary to viral pneumonia.* This is technically accurate. The lungs fail. What the certificate does not record is that the lungs fail *from the inside out*, as if the body simply stopped receiving instruction to maintain them. The neurological architecture that NX-Prime built has no provision for keeping the host alive. It was never designed for a living receiver. It was designed for something that doesn't require a heartbeat.

> *The six dead children were successful connections. The signal that came back through them is now in the air. It is not spreading biologically. It is spreading the way a radio broadcast spreads. Distance is irrelevant. Shielding is irrelevant. The bore site is still transmitting. And the bore site just received six confirmations that the frequency works.*

### 〔T1-B〕 The Seven-Second Recording
*Trigger: Agents locate and recover the nurse's recording — either from a backup, a secondary device, or a forensic recovery of the erased file.*

The file is 7.3 seconds long. Audio quality is poor — fluorescent hospital ambience, the beep of a pulse-ox monitor, a hushed "oh god" from off-camera. The child in frame is a girl, six years old, lying in the hospital bed. Her eyes are open. Her chest moves. Her mouth is moving.

The sound that comes out does not match the mouth movements. The mouth moves in the pattern of a child saying something simple, something short. The sound that comes out is longer. It overlaps itself. It is — and every Agent who hears it will feel this the same way, without being able to explain why — *pointed directly at them*. Like a flashlight sweeping a dark room that stops when it finds you.

> **SAN Loss:** 1/1D4 **(U)** — the sound is wrong in a way that bypasses analysis and lands somewhere older.

> **Handler Note:** Any Agent who succeeds on a **Psychotherapy** roll after hearing the recording can articulate what disturbed them: "It sounded like a question. And I think it already had the answer." This is flavor. Do not confirm it is accurate.

The specialist says, quietly: *"I've never seen that. I don't think that's supposed to be able to happen."*

> **SAN Loss:** 0/1 **(U)** — the world's rules bent a little, just enough to notice.

An Agent who examines both printouts side-by-side and succeeds on a **SIGINT** or **Science (Physics)** roll notices something that they cannot immediately explain: the waveform pattern is not neurological in origin. ## ⚠️ Handler Notes on SAN Application

**Pace the tiers.** An Agent who has absorbed Tier 1 and Tier 2 hits before the Tier 3 triggers lands arrives at those moments already wounded. The mathematics of *Breaking Points* means that cumulative small losses are as dangerous as a single catastrophic one. Do not rush to the spectacular.

**The signal is not a character.** It does not have motivations in any frame an Agent can access. It does not want anything that maps to human desire. Do not let it speak, explain, or threaten. The horror of T3-C is that the child's grip is gentle and the signal passing through the Agent is *indifferent*. Indifference is more frightening than malice. Malice implies you matter enough to be hated.

**The lingering frequency from T3-C is never resolved.** It is not a mechanic. It does not cause SAN loss. It is simply present — occasionally, in quiet moments — for the rest of the Agent's operational life. If the Agent ever returns to active duty, it is still there. If they retire, it is still there. It is not a corruption. It is a memory of having briefly been in the circuit of something vast. The Agent was not changed. They were simply *noticed.* That is enough.

**The bore site does not close by dying.** CARDINAL LOOP can be burned. The children cannot be un-killed. Agent NORA cannot be un-betrayed. But the bore site is open, and closing it requires something the operation brief did not prepare the Agents for, and that Delta Green does not have a protocol for. The question of how — and whether — it can be closed is the question that will carry the campaign forward, long after Overland Park, long after Wichita, long after the last cover story has been filed with the last surviving witness who was willing to be satisfied with it.

The signal is still broadcasting. The bore site is still open. And somewhere in the Persian Gulf, ships are burning.
