# Operation SHADOW DUST

## Theme
This is 2026. A new COVID variant emerges. Russo-Ukrainian war is a stalemate. The US is in preparation to invade Iran as Iran blocks oil shimpments in Ormuz strait.  World is on the brink of economic collapse.

## Core Premise & Setting
It is March 2026. A new COVID variant — designation SARS-CoV-3 'Lazarus' — has begun spreading from a cluster of cases in Baku, Azerbaijan, near the Iranian border. Markets are in freefall. The Strait of Hormuz has been mined and blockaded by the IRGC Navy, and a US carrier group is steaming toward the Persian Gulf. The Russo-Ukrainian front is frozen in a radioactive no-man's-land of drone swarms and mud. Nobody is watching the quiet places anymore.

The Agents are quietly activated by their Handler after a classified SIGINT intercept — flagged by an NSA algorithm and buried inside a routine FEMA pandemic-preparedness digest — reveals an anomalous signal pattern originating from a decommissioned Soviet-era oil infrastructure platform in the Caspian Sea, known in declassified documents only as OBJEKT-7. The signal is not digital. It is not radio. It is mechanical: a rhythmic, subsonic percussion transmitted through the seabed itself, at a frequency that causes localized hemorrhaging in cetaceans and induces vivid, shared hallucinations in exposed humans within a 40-kilometer radius.

Delta Green's initial assessment is terse: Iranian-backed saboteurs are using experimental acoustic weapons to destabilize Azerbaijani oil infrastructure ahead of the US military buildup. That is the cover story. That is the lie.

The truth, which the Agents will excavate layer by bloody layer, is that OBJEKT-7 was never a Soviet oil platform. It was a containment shell — a massive, industrial-era mechanical apparatus constructed between 1949 and 1957 under NKVD oversight, built around something the Soviets found at the bottom of the Caspian: a pre-human ritual engine. A device of interlocking iron rings, oscillating brass pistons, and gyroscopic chambers inscribed with non-Euclidean geometries that the Soviet engineers mistook for ornamental casting defects. The machine does not run on electricity. It runs on the Ritual — a continuous, self-perpetuating hypergeometric process that began before recorded history and has never fully stopped.

In 1991, when the Soviet Union collapsed, OBJEKT-7's maintenance crew vanished. The machine slowed. The Ritual degraded. Something inside the device began to starve.

In 2024, a small cell of Iranian-backed lore seekers — engineers and theologians operating under a Revolutionary Guard cultural preservation program — rediscovered OBJEKT-7 in declassified Azerbaijani petroleum survey records. They believed they had found a pre-Islamic sacred site. They traveled to the platform. They restarted the machine.

They did not survive to understand what they had done.

The machine is now running at 34% operational capacity and climbing. The subsonic signal is a byproduct — a waste emission. The Lazarus variant is not a byproduct. It is a delivery mechanism: the Ritual requires biological material in a specific state of cellular distress, and the variant is inducing that state in the population of the surrounding region with geometric efficiency. The hemorrhagic signature of 'Lazarus' is not viral in origin. Field autopsies on Baku victims will reveal that the internal damage pattern is consistent with exposure to tightly focused subsonic resonance — not infection.

The cover-up is threefold and already in motion before the Agents arrive. First, the CDC and WHO have classified the Lazarus variant under a standard P3 pandemic protocol, suppressing anomalous autopsy data to avoid 'public panic.' Second, the US DoD has quietly designated OBJEKT-7 as a potential Iranian PSYOP/acoustic weapon site and placed it on the pre-invasion target list — meaning an air strike is being planned that would not destroy the machine but would instead rupture its containment shell and release whatever has been feeding inside it for seventy years. Third, and most critically, there is a fourth party: a small, internal Delta Green cell — designation unknown, access level above the Agents' clearance — that has known about OBJEKT-7 since 2019. They chose not to act. The files they buried are the reason the Iranian lore seekers found it. The Agents will eventually find evidence that the cover-up did not begin with the Iranians, or the Soviets, or the CDC. It began inside Delta Green itself.

## THE CONTACT

The meeting happens in a parking structure in Rosslyn, Virginia. Level P4. You were told to arrive separately, on foot, no phones. The instructions came through the usual dead drop — a FEMA pandemic-preparedness digest, eighty-three pages of boilerplate respiratory triage protocols, and buried on page seventy-one, between a table of ventilator allocation thresholds and a footnote about morgue capacity surges in the Caucasus region, a nine-digit coordinate string and a time.

He is already there when the last of you arrives.

Agent Lincoln is a heavyset man going soft in the middle, the kind of federal director who used to fill a suit correctly and no longer does. The jacket is off-the-rack charcoal, slightly shiny at the elbows, and there is a dark crescent of sweat soaked through the back of his collar despite the cold. His face is broad and flushed — the face of a man who has not slept in three days and has been drinking instead of eating. When he smiles, it arrives too fast and stays too long, and it never touches his eyes, which are doing something else entirely: scanning the ramp, the stairwell door, the ceiling. His fingers, when he opens the manila folio he's carrying, are trembling.

Not from cold.

He does not introduce himself by anything other than the code name. He does not shake hands.

He says: *"You need to understand something before I show you this. I have a daughter at George Mason. I have a son who's nine. I need you to understand that when I tell you this is the most important thing I have ever handed to human beings, I am not speaking operationally."*

He sets the folio on the hood of the nearest car.

*"I am speaking as a father."*

## 🔒 Final Handler Note — What The Subject Is Doing

The subject is not malevolent. It is not benevolent. These are human categories applied to a process that predates the evolution of the nervous system that generates such categories.

The subject is feeding. The Ritual is the feeding mechanism. The machine is the feeding apparatus. The Synchronized are the food — not consumed in a way that kills them quickly, but *processed*: their cellular distress, their synchronized neural resonance, the specific biological state the Lazarus variant induces, is the substrate the Ritual converts into whatever the subject requires to maintain and expand its metabolic synchronization with the engine.

The subject has been doing this since before the engine was on the Caspian seabed.

The subject was doing this before the Soviet engineers built the containment shell.

The Soviet engineers' containment shell slowed the process. The 1991 cessation of maintenance slowed it further. The 34 years of degraded operation left the subject in a state the NKVD would have recognized: not starving, not fed, sustained at subsistence by the residual Ritual process, patient in the way that very old things are patient — not because patience is a virtue but because time, at that scale, is not experienced as deprivation.

The Agents will interrupt the machine. The machine will slow. The Ritual will degrade.

And in the silence after the anticipation sound, in the sudden absence of the subsonic percussion, in the 40-kilometer radius of Baku where the Synchronized will blink and look at each other and not know why they are facing northeast — in all of that silence —

The subject will still be there.

Inside the machine.

Waiting for the next 1991. The next period of degraded maintenance. The next reasonable assessment by a frightened committee that today is not the day to act.

It has done this before.

It will do it again.

The question the Agents cannot answer, and should not be allowed to answer, is: **what is it building toward?**

The machine is not a cage. It is not a feeding tube.

It is both.

It has always been both.
